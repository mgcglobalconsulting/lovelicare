Clinical Reference

Vitamin Cheat Sheet

Your complete guide to vitamins, minerals, and nutrients — organized by wellness goal. Use this to personalize your IV therapy protocol.

✨ Skin, Hair & Nails

Biotin (B7)

Essential for keratin production. Supports stronger hair and nails. Deficiency causes brittle nails and hair thinning.

Vitamin C

Required for collagen synthesis. Helps skin elasticity and wound healing. Antioxidant protecting against UV damage.

Glutathione

Master antioxidant. Improves skin clarity, brightening, and pigmentation issues. Detoxifies at cellular level.

CoQ10

Energizes skin cells and reduces fine lines. Helps maintain firmness and reduce oxidative damage.

Most Impactful Combo

Biotin + Vitamin C + Lysine + Proline + Zinc + Glutathione + CoQ10 — covers collagen production, antioxidant protection, and hair/nail strength.

🧠 Stress, Mood, Sleep & Focus

Magnesium

Calms nervous system via GABA receptors. Deficiency linked to anxiety, irritability, and sleep problems. Supports melatonin.

Vitamin B6 (Pyridoxine)

Produces serotonin, dopamine, and GABA. Supports mood stability and stress resilience.

Taurine

Supports calming GABA neurotransmitter. Reduces anxiety and promotes relaxation. Helps with focus under stress.

Glycine

Lowers core body temperature at night, signaling sleep. Improves sleep onset and quality. Helps mental clarity.

Best Mood & Sleep Combo

Magnesium + B6 + B12 + Folate + Vitamin D + Taurine + Glycine

⚡ Fat Burn, Energy & Metabolism

M.I.C. (Methionine, Inositol, Choline)

Classic lipotropic combo that helps mobilize and metabolize fat in the liver.

L-Carnitine

Shuttles fatty acids into mitochondria so the body can actually burn fat for energy.

B12 (Methylcobalamin)

Boosts energy production, supports metabolism, and enhances fat mobilization when paired with MIC.

Alpha Lipoic Acid (ALA)

Potent antioxidant improving insulin sensitivity and fat utilization. Also regenerates Glutathione and Vitamin C.

Fastest Fat-Burn Combo

MIC + Carnitine + B12 + ALA + Chromium + Vitamin D = more energy, fewer cravings, faster results.

🛡️ Detox & Immunity

Glutathione (Master Antioxidant)

Neutralizes toxins, critical for liver detox Phase II. Protects immune cells and enhances their ability to fight pathogens.

NAC (N-Acetylcysteine)

Precursor to glutathione — replenishes liver detox reserves. Excellent for long-term liver protection.

Zinc

Critical for white blood cell development. Helps shorten duration of colds/viruses. Deficiency increases infection risk.

Selenium

Works with glutathione enzymes to enhance detox capacity. Supports thyroid detoxification.

Core Detox Combo

Glutathione + ALA + Vitamin C + Zinc + Vitamin D — the five essentials, supported by B vitamins, Selenium, and Taurine.
